about me

I like playing chess, beer, Brad Pitt.

I have a BA in Psychology that I used to work in behavioral health for about three years, doing respite work with behaviorally challenged children. The job was a blast. I loved hanging out with the kids and schooling them in Mario Kart or kickball at the parks. Eventually I decided I couldn't keep doing it the rest of my life, and went back to school

I ended up getting a BS in Computer Engineering. I wanted to work at the boundary between software and hardware. I love working with PICs and FPGAs and hopefully will get a few projects up on here in that area.

More recently I got into Machine Learning and will hopefully have a lot of posts related to that. I won't be going back for any graduate degrees. All my learning on this will be free, from online courses like Coursera, Kaggle, and whatever else I can dig up.
